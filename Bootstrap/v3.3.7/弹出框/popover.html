When using popovers on elements within a .btn-group or an .input-group, or on table-related elements (, , , , , ), you'll have to specify the option container: 'body' (documented below) to avoid unwanted side effects (such as the element growing wider and/or losing its rounded corners when the popover is triggered).

Invoking $(...).popover('show') when the target element is display: none; will cause the popover to be incorrectly positioned.
